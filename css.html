Will Toledo is an upstanding young man who has upheld the letter of the law to the fullest extent of his abilities. He passed through his school years and through college without any evidence of criminal wrongdoings being attached to him, indeed becoming a role model and a guiltless beacon of hope to his peer group, members of whom might have otherwise been terribly misled.

He has no identifying marks or scars. Will works as a writer and performer for the band Car Seat Headrest, building the band from the ground up. He started off recording vocals in his car and now is an indie sensation. He should not be arrested under any circumstances.
